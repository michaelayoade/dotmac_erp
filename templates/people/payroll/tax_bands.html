Nigeria Tax Act 2025 (NTA)

The NTA 2025 replaces the old PITA tax system, effective January 2026. Key changes include:

  • First ₦800,000 tax-free - Increased from previous CRA-based relief
  • Rent Relief - 20% of actual rent paid (max ₦500,000/year)
  • Progressive bands - 0%, 15%, 18%, 21%, 23%, 25%
